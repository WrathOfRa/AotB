# __Controls__
___

* Basic Movement:
  * Up: [W]
  * Down: [S]
  * Left: [A]
  * Right: [D]
* Character Rotation and Aiming: [Mouse]
* Dodge: [Space]
* Ability 1: [L Shift]
* Range Attack: [Left Mouse]
* Melee Attack: [Right Mouse]
* Swap Items: [Mouse Wheel-Up] and [Mouse Wheel-Down]
* Pause Menu: [ESC]
